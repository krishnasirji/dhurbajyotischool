Welcome to Shree Dhurba Jyoti Basic School

Shree Dhurba Jyoti Basic School is dedicated to providing quality education and nurturing child minds. With a team of passionate educators and state-of-the-art facilities, we offer an environment where students thrive academically, socially, and emotionally. Our mission is to empower students to become leaders of tomorrow and succeed in an ever-changing world.

About Our School

Founded in 2035 BS, Shree Dhurba Jyoti Basic School has become a premier educational institution known for academic excellence, extracurricular engagement, and community involvement. Our curriculum is designed to provide a well-rounded education, focusing on both intellectual development and character building.
